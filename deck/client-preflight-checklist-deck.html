PHASE 01 · TELEGRAM

Your Telegram Bot

Telegram is how you talk to your agent. Before setup day, you need to create a bot and keep its token somewhere safe — that's the only piece we need from you.

HOW TO CREATE YOUR BOT

1

Open Telegram and go to @BotFather

Search for @BotFather in the Telegram search bar and open that chat.

2

Send the command /newbot

Follow the prompts. You'll choose a name and a username for your bot.

3

Copy the token BotFather gives you

It looks like 1234567890:ABCDEFghijklmnop. Save it somewhere safe — you'll hand it to us on setup day.

↗ OPEN BOTFATHER ON TELEGRAM

WHAT IS THIS TOKEN FOR?

It connects the agent to Telegram

The token is the "key" that authorizes your agent to send and receive messages through your bot. Without it, the gateway between OpenClaw and Telegram cannot be activated.

IMPORTANT

Keep the token private. Do not share it in public channels or screenshots. Send it to us only through a secure method during setup day.

WHAT ABOUT THE FORUM?

You do not need to create any Telegram groups, forums, or topics before setup day. We create everything together after we know how you want to operate. The only thing you need is: bot created + token saved.

PHASE 02 of 06

Baseline Install

This is what we do. You just need to be available and ready to approve system prompts.

WHAT GETS INSTALLED

OpenClaw + Concierge

The core system that lets your agent think, remember, and act. Lives entirely on your Mac mini.

Dedicated Agent Browser

A browser with a separate profile used only by the agent. Operational accounts stay logged in there — keeps sessions clean and separate from yours.

Mission Control Dashboard

A local dashboard where you can see what your agent is doing, what's running, and what's coming next.

Tailscale + Remote Access

Secure remote connection for ongoing support. You can revoke it at any time from your Tailscale account.

WHAT YOU DON'T NEED TO DO

  • ✕ Install any software manually
  • ✕ Configure any files or settings
  • ✕ Set up Telegram groups or forums yet
  • ✕ Know how the system works under the hood

PHASE 04 of 06

Discovery

A structured conversation between you and your agent. It gathers what it needs to understand how to serve you — and decides together what areas to activate first.

WHAT THE AGENT ASKS ABOUT

[→]

How do you currently work?

Your tools, rhythms, communication habits, and where friction usually shows up.

[→]

What takes too much of your time?

Tasks that feel repetitive, manual, or that regularly fall through the cracks.

[→]

What would a first win look like?

A concrete result in the first week that would make you say: "okay, this was worth it."

[→]

What are your active operating areas?

Projects, clients, teams, or responsibilities that need real attention — not just ideas.

OUTPUT OF THIS PHASE

A clear list of Fronts

By the end of this conversation, we know exactly which areas of your life or work deserve their own dedicated space in the system. These become your first operating Fronts.

IMPORTANT

We do not create Telegram groups or topics before this conversation. The structure only makes sense after we know what needs to exist.

HOW LONG?

Approximately 20–40 minutes of honest conversation. Your agent guides the whole thing — you just answer.

CONTINUE ↓

PHASE 05 of 06

Operational Structure

Now we build the actual structure. This only happens after Discovery — because it's shaped entirely by what you told us.

WE CREATE TOGETHER

[→]

Your Telegram Forum Group

A private supergroup set up as a forum — this is where all real agent operations will happen.

[→]

Mission Control Topic

A dedicated thread called 00-mission-control for coordination, intake, and priorities.

[→]

One Topic per Front

Each operating area identified in Discovery gets its own thread. Each one is isolated, focused, and fully linked to a workspace folder.

THE RULE THAT MAKES IT WORK

One Topic
= One Front

Each thread in Telegram maps to a folder in the workspace. That's what keeps memory, context, and outputs clean — even months from now.

Need a new Front later? You ask your agent from 00-mission-control and it creates everything for you.
